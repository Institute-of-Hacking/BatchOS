BatchOS is a project dedicated to no specific purpose. It's just people who are bored that wanted to do something. Anyway, I wanted to make an OS that could be run on any windows system that supported Batchfile, which is a lot, so I made this, a text-based OS that isn't Unix based. I can't believe it either.
